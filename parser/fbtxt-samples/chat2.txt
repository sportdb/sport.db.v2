###########
## 10 score formats
#################


######
## 1. Standard result (most common worldwide)
##
## TEAM_A 2–1 TEAM_B

SPAIN 2–1 ITALY

## =>
##  TEAM_A
##  FT_SCORE_A
##  FT_SCORE_B
##  TEAM_B

########
## 2. Team-per-line newspaper format
##
## TEAM_A (HT) FT
## TEAM_B (HT) FT

URUGUAY (2) 4
ARGENTINA (1) 3

## =>
##  TEAM
##  HT_SCORE
##  FT_SCORE


####
##  3. Inline half-time score
##
## TEAM_A 4–3 TEAM_B (2–1)

URUGUAY 4–3 ARGENTINA (2–1)

## =>
## FT 4–3
## HT 2–1


###
##  4. Extra-time indicator
##
##  TEAM_A 2–1 TEAM_B aet

SPAIN 2–1 ITALY (aet)

## =>
## FT = 1–1    xxxx - note - score missing!
## ET = 2–1
## 
## Common markers - aet / et /after extra time


###
## 5. Extra-time explicit score
##
##  TEAM_A 1–1 TEAM_B (aet 2–1)

SPAIN 1–1 ITALY (aet 2–1)

## => 
## FT = 1–1
## ET = 2–1


###
## 6. Penalty shootout (bracket form)
##
##  TEAM_A 1–1 TEAM_B (5–4 pens)

ITALY 1–1 SPAIN (5–4 pens)

## =>
## FT = 1–1
## PEN = 5–4
##
##  Common tokens -  pens / pen / p / penalties


#####
## 7. Penalties embedded per team
##
## TEAM_A 1 (5) TEAM_B 1 (4)

ITALY 1 (5) SPAIN 1 (4)

## =>
## FT = 1–1    -   what about extra time??
## PEN = 5–4


####
## 8. Aggregate score (two-leg ties)
##
##  TEAM_A 2–1 TEAM_B (agg 4–3)

REAL MADRID 2–1 BAYERN (agg 4–3)

## =>
##  MATCH_SCORE = 2–1
##  AGGREGATE_SCORE = 4–3
##
##  Common tokens -  agg / aggregate / on agg


####
## 9. Away-goals decision
##
## Older competitions sometimes show:
##
## TEAM_A 1–1 TEAM_B (agg 2–2, away goals)

## CHELSEA 1–1 PSG (agg 2–2, PSG win on away goals)

CHELSEA 1–1 PSG (agg 2–2, win on away goals)

CHELSEA 1–1 PSG (agg 2–2) 
[PSG win on away goals]



###
##  10. Forfeit / awarded match
##
## Sometimes written:
##
## TEAM_A 3–0 TEAM_B (awarded)
##  or
##  TEAM_A w/o TEAM_B

TEAM A 3–0 TEAM B  [awarded]
TEAM A w/o TEAM B

# additional examples in the team-per-line newspaper style
####

## 1. Penalty shootout (note after the two lines)
ITALY      (0) 1
SPAIN      (0) 1
[Italy win 5–4 pens]

ITALY      1-1 (0-0), 5-4 pens  SPAIN
ITALY v SPAIN        1-1 (0-0), 5-4 pens 


## 2. Penalty shootout with abbreviation
BRAZIL     (1) 2
URUGUAY    (1) 2
[Brazil won 4–3 pens]

BRAZIL     2-2 (1-1), 4-3 pens  URUGUAY
BRAZIL v URUGUAY    2-2 (1-1), 4-3 pens  
 

## 3. Penalties with minimal wording
GERMANY    (0) 0
FRANCE     (0) 0
[France win pens 3–2]

GERMANY   0-0 (0-0), 2-3 pen. FRANCE 
GERMANY  v FRANCE     0-0 (0-0), 2-3 pen. 


## 4. Walkover (forfeit)
PORTUGAL   w/o
GREECE

PORTUGAL   w/o  GREECE
PORTUGAL v GREECE  [walkover]


## 5. Walkover with score awarded
##
## Some competitions officially record:

PORTUGAL   3
GREECE     0
[walkover]

PORTUGAL   3-0 GREECE   [walkover]
PORTUGAL   3-0 awd. GREECE  [walkover]


## 6. Walkover noted after the result

NETHERLANDS 3
ICELAND     0
[Iceland forfeited]

NETHERLANDS 3-0 ICELAND  [Iceland forfeited]
NETHERLANDS 3-0 awd. ICELAND 
NETHERLANDS 3-0 ICELAND [awarded]


## 7. Abandoned match (after score shown)

ARGENTINA  (1) 2
CHILE      (0) 1
[match abandoned]

ARGENTINA   2-1 (1-0) CHILE   [match abandoned]
ARGENTINA   2-1 (1-0) CHILE   [abandoned]
ARGENTINA   2-1 abd. CHILE

ARGENTINA  (1) 2

[abandoned 68 mins]

ARGENTINA  2-1 (1-0) CHILE   [abandoned 68 mins]
ARGENTINA  2-1 abd. CHILE   [abandoned at 68']
ARGENTINA  2-1 abd. CHILE   [at 68']
