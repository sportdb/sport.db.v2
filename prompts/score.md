- Standard Format: 1–1 (4–5 pen)

### 2. Sudden-Death Shootout Notation
A shootout moves into "sudden death" if teams are still tied after the initial five kicks each. In sudden death, the shootout ends as soon as one team scores and the other misses in the same round. 

- Notation Style: There is no special shorthand for "sudden death" in the scoreline; you simply list the final high score.
- Example: 3–3 (9–8 pen)
  - A high penalty score like 9–8 or 11–10 implicitly tells the reader that the shootout went into sudden death rounds.
